Venue

Mountain Springs Lodge in Plain, Washington

Holiday Weekend

The wedding is July 6th, but the fun will start a few days earlier on July 4th. Hiking, ziplining, and whitewater rafting are just a few of the fun things to do in the area and we hope you'll join us for the whole weekend.

Where to stay?

We've reserved several of the cabins at Mountain Springs for immediate family and the wedding party to give the wedding an all inclusive feel. Everyone is invited to enjoy the amenities while we're there.

Beaver Valley Lodge

Mention Brown-McDevitt wedding. There is a block of twelve rooms specifically reserved for the wedding weekend. 2 night minimum stay. Only 0.7 miles away from the venue!

Tamarack Vacation Rentals

Several cabins for rent in the area. The manager also works at Mountain Springs and gave us the tour of the venu. She's very nice!

AirBnB / VRBO

Look for a place to stay in Plain Washington, which is closer to the venue. Nearby Leavenworth also has lots of options.

Natapoc Lodging

Another nearby resort with cabins to rent.

Travel

Mountain Springs Lodge is about a two hour drive from Seattle. If you're coming in from out of the state, we recommend flying in to Sea-Tac airport and renting a car. There is an airport in Wenatchee, WA that is about an hour closer, but flights into Wenatchee often get canceled and they almost all fly through Seattle anyway.
